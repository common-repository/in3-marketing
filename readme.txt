Let your customers immediately see the purchase price &quot;divided by three&quot; on the product page, shopping cart or check-out.

== Description ==

With iDEAL in3, you let your customers pay in three equal instalments without interest or negative registration. This lowers the purchase threshold resulting in an increase of conversion and average order amount. The earlier iDEAL in3 is present in the customer journey, the greater the success in increasing conversion.

With the on-site messaging plug-in of iDEAL in3 for WooCommerce, your customers will immediately see the purchase price &quot;divided by three&quot; on the product page, shopping cart or check-out.

* Customers will immediately see the &lsquo;&rsquo;instalment price&rsquo;&rsquo; for products over &euro;100
* Effectively increase conversion and increase the average order amount
* Applicable for simple, variable and grouped products
* Installed within seconds

After installation, you will find the settings in the WooCommerce sidebar. Questions about the plug-in or its installation? Please visit <a href="https://www.payin3.eu/" rel="noopener noreferrer" target="_blank">www.payin3.eu</a>.

=== Minimum Requirements ===

* WooCommerce 5.9 or greater
* WordPress 5.4 or greater
* PHP version 7.2 or greater

=== Installation ===

1. Upload the plugin files to the `/wp-content/plugins/in3` directory.
1. Activate the plugin through the `Plugins` screen in WordPress
1. Proceed with the plugin's settings in admin area under `WooCommerce -> iDEAL in3 Marketing Tool` admin menu
